Old Fashioned

All-weather, pack-a-punch, eternal classic! Simplicity is power.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp simple syrup
  • 1 tsp water
  • 2 dashes bitters
  • 50 ml bourbon whiskey
  • 1 orange slice
  • 1 maraschino cherry
  • some ice

Steps

  1. Pour the simple syrup, water, and bitters into a whiskey glass. Stir to combine, then place the ice cubes in the glass
  2. Pour bourbon over the ice, and garnish with the orange slice and maraschino cherry.

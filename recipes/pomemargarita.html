Pomegranate Margarita

Ay Dios Mio, solo quiero ser popular! Bebida perfecta para el verano.

Ingredients

  • 120ml tequila
  • 60ml Triple Sec
  • half-cup pomegranate juice
  • 1 juiced lime
  • club soda or tonic water
  • some ice
  • salt
  • lime wedge, to serve

Steps

  1. Pour salt onto a plate or shallow dish.
  2. With a lime wedge, wet the rim of a glass, then dip the glass onto the salted plate to coat the rim.
  3. Fill the glass with ice.
  4. In a cocktail shaker with ice, pour in the tequila, triple sec, pomegranate juice, and lime juice. Shake.
  5. Pour into the glass then top off with club soda. Garnish with a lime wedge.

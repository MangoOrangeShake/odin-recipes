Bloody Mary

A savory, flavory smasher that'll get you going the whole night longgg... or at least till your husband picks you up.

Ingredients

  • some ice
  • 100ml vodka
  • 500ml tomato juice
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Worcestershire or 'Wooster' sauce
  • Tabasco
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 celery sticks, to serve
  • 2 lemon slices, to serve

Steps

  1. Place the ice in a large glass. Measure the vodka, tomato juice and lemon juice and pour it straight onto the ice.
  2. Add 3 shakes of 'Wooster' sauce and Tabasco and a pinch of salt and pepper. Stir until the glass feels chilly.
  3. Top up with fresh ice, add celery sticks and a lemon slice, and voila!
